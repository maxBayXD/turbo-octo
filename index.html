Our Story

Passionate about visual storytelling, we combine technical expertise with artistic vision.

Founded in 2015 by award-winning colorist James Wilson, Hues has grown from a one-person operation to a full-service post-production studio serving clients worldwide. Our team of editors, colorists, and VFX artists share a common goal: to bring your creative vision to life with unparalleled quality and attention to detail.

We believe post-production is more than just technical work—it's the final stage of storytelling where raw footage transforms into a polished, emotionally resonant experience. Whether you're an independent filmmaker or a global brand, we treat every project with the care and dedication it deserves.

Our boutique approach means you work directly with senior artists who understand your goals and can execute at the highest level. No junior operators, no assembly-line workflows—just premium craftsmanship from start to finish.
